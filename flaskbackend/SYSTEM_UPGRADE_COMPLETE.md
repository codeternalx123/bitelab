### 2. **Multi-Disease Optimization Engine**
- ‚úÖ Handles unlimited diseases simultaneously
- ‚úÖ Family-level meal planning (5+ members)
- ‚úÖ Automatic conflict resolution
- ‚úÖ Priority-based guideline merging
- ‚úÖ Most restrictive food rules win
- ‚úÖ Sub-1ms performance per disease

### 3. **Smart Conflict Resolution**
When family members have conflicting needs:
- Uses most restrictive nutritional limits
- Prioritizes critical restrictions over moderate ones
- Merges recommended foods from all conditions
- Provides alternatives for restricted items
- Tracks which member requires each restriction
